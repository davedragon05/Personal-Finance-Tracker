How it works

1. Create a DEBT sub-category for each loan or person (e.g. "Juan Loan", "Car Loan").

2. Choose the type: "I am paying my loan" (your own debt) or "Someone is paying me back" (money owed to you).

3. Record the Outstanding Balance and Amount Paid/Received.

4. Payments auto-create transactions in the Ledger — red for debt payments, green for repayments received.

5. When the balance reaches zero, it shows "Fully Paid".
